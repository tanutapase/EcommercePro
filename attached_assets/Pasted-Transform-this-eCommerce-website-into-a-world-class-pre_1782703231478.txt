Transform this eCommerce website into a world-class premium modern shopping experience. Keep the design minimal, luxurious, elegant, lightning-fast, and production-ready. Do NOT create a generic template. The final result should feel like a combination of Apple, Nike, Nothing, Linear, Vercel, Arc Browser, Shopify, and premium SaaS products.

## Overall Design Philosophy

* Premium minimalism
* Lots of whitespace
* Pixel-perfect alignment
* Modern typography (Inter or Geist)
* Rounded corners (16–24px)
* Glassmorphism where appropriate
* Soft shadows only
* Smooth gradients
* Micro interactions everywhere
* Fully responsive
* Mobile-first optimization
* Accessibility friendly
* Fast loading
* Clean animations
* High quality icons (Lucide)
